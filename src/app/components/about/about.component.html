About

Education

The word blockchain has been used heavily in the past couple years. The SMU Blockchain Society teaches its members about why it became so relevant, how it is being misintrepreted, and when to use it. Understanding the use cases of blockchain technology is crucial information needed before deciding to build or invest in this space.

Training

SMU Blockchain Society trains its members to have a broad understanding of the field. The Society focuses on two major aspects: Investment & Development. The Development Committee focuses on training the engineer oriented students on building decentralized applications and smart contracts. The Investments Committee focuses on the cryptocurrency market, hosts trading competitions, and analyzes chart patterns.

Preparation

Members of the SMU Blockchain Society will be prepared for job recruitment within their respective majors regarding the blockchain space. They will have been to mulitple conferences and be able to present what they have accomplished with tact during the job recruitment process.
